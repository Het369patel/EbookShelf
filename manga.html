Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is a TV series about a weak hunter named Sung Jinwoo who gains extraordinary powers through a mysterious program and becomes one of the strongest hunters. The story takes place in a world where humans have magical abilities and must battle monsters to protect the human race.

Bungo Stray Dogs

The show is about an orphan named Atsushi Nakajima who is kicked out of his orphanage and rescues a man named Dazai Osamu from committing suicide. Dazai is a member of a detective agency with supernatural powers that handles cases too dangerous for the police or military. Atsushi learns he has a supernatural ability that allows him to transform into a berserker white tiger.

Horimiya

Horimiya is a high school romance anime series that follows the friendship between Kyoko Hori, a popular student, and Izumi Miyamura, a shy and introverted classmate. The two develop a story of school life after meeting outside of class.
